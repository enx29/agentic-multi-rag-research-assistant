Engineering Economics

Engineering Economics helps evaluate financial decisions in engineering projects.

Capital Budgeting Techniques

Net Present Value (NPV)

NPV is the difference between the present value of cash inflows and cash outflows.

Decision Rule:
NPV > 0 → Accept
NPV < 0 → Reject

Internal Rate of Return (IRR)

IRR is the discount rate at which NPV becomes zero.

Payback Period

The time required to recover the initial investment.

Benefit Cost Ratio (BCR)

BCR = Benefits / Costs

Decision Rule:
BCR > 1 → Accept

Replacement Analysis

Used to determine whether an existing asset should be replaced.

Sensitivity Analysis

Studies how changes in variables affect project outcomes.
